About Me

I am Nihit Srivastava, a budding filmmaker and creative enthusiast currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Mass Communication/Media Studies at Shoolini University. My journey is marked by a passion for visual storytelling and a commitment to diverse creative roles. As the Founder and Head of "Cinedicate," I've led a dynamic filmmaking club, orchestrating collaborations among 90+ members and achieving recognition for our short film, "Ladkhadate Kadam."

Additionally, my experiences in social media management, graphic design, and video editing have shaped my versatile skill set. As I navigate the realms of academia and creative expression, my journey continues to unfold, promising a future filled with exciting narratives and visual wonders
